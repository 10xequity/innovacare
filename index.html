What we do

Four things, done all the way through.

01

Cost takeout

We find the spend that isn't buying anything — in labor models, supply chains, contracts, and the way work is actually done — and we take it out without cutting into care.
02

Interim & fractional leadership

A seat opens — CFO, COO, CNO, CMO — or a function needs a steady hand part-time. We fill it with an operator who has done the job, not a placeholder.
03

Operational optimization

Throughput, staffing, service-line performance, patient flow. We work the floor and the numbers together, because one without the other doesn't hold.
04

New business models

New service lines, market entry, biotech commercialization, provider-payer arrangements. We design the model and then we help you stand it up.
