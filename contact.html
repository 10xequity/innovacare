First contact

Tell us what isn't working.

Everything is confidential from the first message. Name the situation and the outcome you'd want. If we can help, we'll say how — if we can't, we'll point you to someone who can.

What to include

The more specific you are, the better our first conversation. A few useful things to mention:

  • The services or areas you're interested in
  • Your organization and where it sits (system, biotech, PE)
  • The situation and what you'd want to change
  • Any timing or constraints we should know
